What is DOTA2

Defense of the Ancients 2 is a free-to-play multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game developed and published by Valve Corporation. DOTA2 Official Website is here.

In the game, there are two team and each of them has five players. Two teams are fighting against each other to win the battle, basically, destroying their oppnonents’ facilities (e.g., the towers), and the objective is to destroy the main sturcture named “Ancient”. Each team has one “Ancient” and they need to protect it from being attacked by the enemies while attacking their enemies’ facilities.

The development of DOTA refer to Wikipedia.

Heros

Each player need to control one of the “heros” who usually has four special skills, and there are more than 100 heroes that could be chosen. Usually, the heroes are categorized into three different types: Tank, Carry and Support, each has its own job during the battle.

  • For Tank, they are either very “tanky” (hard to be taken down) or have some special skills to absorb demage, and they need to attract enimies attentions as well as protect allies from getting hurt during the battle.
  • And for Carry, they are usually weak and vulnerable at the begining. However, they will gradually become more powerful and be the key to win the game.
  • In terms of Support, there are also different types of them as well, supporting the whole team in vary aspects. They could be doctors who heal their allies, pusher who help the team to destroy enemies towers and ones who get special skills to restrict enemies moves.

Technically, a good team should contain heroes in all these categories since that might help the team during most of the difficulties.

Terms in Game

Personally, the most common terms I use in the game are:

  • Mid, Top, Bottom: Three lines in the map, Middle line, Top line and Bottom line
  • Farm: That means "don't fight with other players, just kills the NPC to get bonus"
  • GG, GGWP: Usually be used at the end of the game, mean "good game, well play"
  • Solo: the player who said that want to take a line individually
  • TP: Town Portal Scroll, a item could bring you to a certain place immediately

More terms refer to DOTA2 Wiki.
